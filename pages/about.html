About Canyon State Roofing

For nearly twenty years, Canyon State Roofing been a specialized provider of quality commercial and residential roofing services in the Phoenix area. We service roofing projects for a variety of property types, including health care facilities, mining facilities, multi-family buildings, hotels, offices, retail, residential homes, new construction, and more.

Whether you are a business owner, property manager, or homeowner in Arizona, we can provide you with the expert roofing services you need. We install commercial roofing systems that come with up to a 15-Year Warranty, and we provide LIFETIME Warranties on each of our Residential Roofs! Give us a call today to learn how we can provide your structure with a long-lasting roofing solution. We proudly perform work in the following Arizona cities: Gilbert, Mesa, Chandler, Apache Junction, Scottsdale, Phoenix, Fountain Hills, Glendale, Deer Valley, Anthem, Sun City, Sun Lakes, Queen Creek, Paradise Valley, Laveen, Goodyear, & Ahwatukee!

Please check out our A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau! Canyon State Roofing has been working hard become the most respected of all commercial roofing contractors in Phoenix, AZ. Our office is headquartered in Gilbert, Arizona, but we do work all over the Phoenix Valley.
